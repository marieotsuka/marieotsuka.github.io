RISD in Rome

Since the inception of the European Honors Program in 1960, RISD has maintained a presence in Rome. The Palazzetto Cenci has been the focal point of every RISD student’s experience in Rome.

Learn Abroad

We work with faculty, faculty committees, student, and leadership to create and support learning opportunities for RISD students beond campus: RISD in Rome: European Honors Program & Summer program, Wintersession travel course, and Exchange Programs with partner schools across the globe.
