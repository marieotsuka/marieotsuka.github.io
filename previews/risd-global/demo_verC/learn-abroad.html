We work with faculty, faculty committees, student, and leadership to create and support learning opportunities for RISD students beyond campus.

RISD Global works with with faculty, faculty committees, student, and leadership to create and support learning opportunities for RISD students beyond campus: RISD in Rome: European Honors Program & Summer program, Wintersession travel course, and Exchange Programs with partner schools across the globe and including North America.

European Honors Program

The European Honors Program is a unique off-campus residency program that welcomes a select group of RISD junior, senior, and grad students to spend a full semester immersed in a global environment.

EHP

Semester Exchange Programs

RISD Global Exchange is an opportunity for RISD students to spend a full semester at one of our 40+ partner schools around the world.

Semester Exchange

Summer Studies Travel Courses

RISD Global offers creative and culturally immersive art and design courses each summer in diverse locations around Europe, Asia, and Africa. The 3-week Global Summer Studies courses are led by RISD faculty and are open to all art and design students.

Summer Studies

Wintersession Travel Courses

For five weeks between the fall and spring semesters, RISD Global offers undergraduate and graduate students rich global learning opportunities through Wintersession Travel Courses.

Wintersession

Global Field Trips

Global field trips are travel learning experiences embedded within on-campus studios, courses and seminar taught on campus during Fall and Spring semester.

Global Field
