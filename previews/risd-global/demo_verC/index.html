RISD GLOBAL develops, facilitates and supports off-campus learning programs and activities, global research and teaching, and projects and initiatives that advance global conversations in art and design.

RISD in Rome

Since the inception of the European Honors Program in 1960, RISD has maintained a presence in Rome. The Palazzetto Cenci has been the focal point of every RISD student’s experience in Rome.
