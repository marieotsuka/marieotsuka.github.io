Marie Otsuka is a designer working on and around methods for making: fonts, websites, tools, and books. She is currently drawing type and programming scripts at Occupant Fonts while teaching at the Rhode Island School of Design. She also works as an independent graphic designer and web developer, including ongoing design and development for Low-tech Magazine. She holds an MFA in Graphic Design from RISD and a BA in Interdisciplinary Studies from the University of Chicago.
