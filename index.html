Marie Otsuka is a designer whose work examines tools and methods for making. She is currently drawing type and programming scripts at Occupant Fonts. She also works as an independent graphic designer and web developer as a member of the Design Office, collaborating with a wide range of organizations, authors, artists, and designers. Marie holds an MFA in Graphic Design from the Rhode Island School of Design and a BA in Interdisciplinary Studies from the University of Chicago.
