Marie Otsuka is a designer working around tools and methods for making. She is currently drawing type and programming scripts at Occupant Fonts. She also works as an independent graphic designer and web developer as a member of the Design Office, collaborating with a wide range of organizations, authors, artists, and designers. Her most recent project with Low-tech Magazine has been featured on publications such as The Gradient of the Walker Arts Center, AIGA Eye on Design, Communication Arts, Fast Company, Motherboard, and Hacker News. Marie holds an MFA in Graphic Design from the Rhode Island School of Design and a BA in Interdisciplinary Studies from the University of Chicago.
